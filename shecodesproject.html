My Purpose Talk Show is a vision I received from God in 2018. The aim of the show is to teach people how to discover God's purpose for their lives by teaching them purpose discovery and fulfillment from God's word .
The show runs on a weekly basis whereby recorded videos are posted on my youtube channel. I usually invite guest speakers on the show to speak on various topics regarding purpose and I also have solo sessions on the show.
